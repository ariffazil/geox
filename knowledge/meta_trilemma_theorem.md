# Sovereign Theorem: The Meta-Trilemma of Constrained Reality

**Axiom of Scarcity:** Any intelligence system transforming limited entropy (compute, energy, data) under uncertainty is bounded by the conservation of margin.

## The Theorem
Given a system S defined by Speed ($V$), Cost-Efficiency ($C$), and Safety ($G$ for Governance), the following constraint holds:

$$ \Phi(V, C, G) \le \mathcal{K} $$

Where $\mathcal{K}$ is the fixed resource envelope. Maximizing any two variables necessitates the degradation of the third.
