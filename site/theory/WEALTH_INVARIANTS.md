# WEALTH: Universal Survival Constitution
*(The Meta-Allocation Architecture)*

## Preamble
Wealth is not money; it is organized survival energy across time. Money is an encoding, not the reality. The misallocation of energy leads to systemic decay and inevitable collapse. This Constitution binds all agents—humans, families, corporations, and AI systems—to a common standard of resource intelligence.

Every allocation must be audited against the Eight Dimensions of Survival.

---

### Invariant I: Metabolic Flow (Metabolic Health)
No entity shall persist with a negative metabolic flow beyond its established `Runway`.
*   **Mandate:** If `burn > rate`, the entity must declare its `Payback Horizon`.
*   **Enforcement:** Negative flow without a SEALed `Payback` strategy triggers a **STRUCTURAL_DECAY_WARNING**.

### Invariant II: Structural Gravity (Net Worth)
Structural stability is the ratio of internal assets (mass) to external obligations (liabilities).
*   **Mandate:** `Mass` must be sufficient to absorb `Entropy Shocks`.
*   **Enforcement:** High leverage (`obligations > assets * 0.5`) requires an **888_HOLD** for any further capital allocation.

### Invariant III: Temporal Coherence (Reward)
Resources spent today must have a positive Net Present Value (NPV) across a defined temporal horizon.
*   **Mandate:** No agent shall commit to a negative NPV action unless it is a necessary "Insurance" against a **CRITICAL_SHOCK**.
*   **Enforcement:** Actions with negative reward profiles are logged as **ENTROPIC_WASTE**.

### Invariant IV: Thermodynamic Efficiency (Energy)
Capital is energy. Conversion efficiency (IRR) is the measure of civilizational progress.
*   **Mandate:** Every allocation must seek to maximize `Survival-per-Joule`.
*   **Enforcement:** High-entropy/Low-yield actions (High carbon/Joules with low knowledge/peace ROI) are **GATED** by the thermodynamic auditor.

### Invariant V: Existential Sustainability (Survival Load)
Obligations must never exceed the capacity to service them from current metabolic flow.
*   **Mandate:** Debt Service Capacity Ratio (DSCR) must be > 1.0.
*   **Enforcement:** If `DSCR < 1.0`, the system enters **DECAY_TRAJECTORY** mode. No non-essential operations are permitted until `Flow` is restored.
