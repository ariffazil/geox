### 2. State Estimation and Inversion Governance

**GEOX MCP as governed broker between observations and inferred Earth state.**

### 3. Derived-Property Engines

Since permeability, moduli, brittleness, saturation, impedance are **not in canon**, GEOX MCP computes them through **governed kernels**.

| Derived | From Canon-9 | Operator |
|---------|--------------|----------|
| **K, μ, E** | ρ, Vp, Vs | Elastic moduli equations |
| **Permeability** | φ, fabric, stress, facies | Constitutive model |
| **Fluid mobility** | φ, P, T, ρₑ | Multi-phase flow |
| **Thermal diffusivity** | k, ρ, Cp model | Heat equation |
| **Storage capacity** | φ, P, T + geometry | Volumetrics |

**This creates a plugin physics economy:**
- Different operators for carbonates
- Different operators for shales
- Different operators for fractured systems
- **All staying downstream from one canonical state**
